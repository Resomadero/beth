About Salay

Discover the rich heritage and vibrant community of Salay, a coastal municipality with a blend of natural wonders and cultural treasures.

Our Rich History

Founded in 1858, Salay is one of the oldest settlements in Misamis Oriental. The name "Salay" is derived from the word "Salai," which means to hang fish to dry - a testament to the town's fishing heritage.

Over the centuries, Salay has evolved from a small fishing village to a thriving municipality while preserving its cultural identity and natural resources. The town is known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial houses, historic church, and traditional practices.

Today, Salay is a progressive municipality that balances economic development with environmental conservation and cultural preservation. Our community takes pride in our heritage while embracing opportunities for sustainable growth.
